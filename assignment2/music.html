Music is such a huge part of my life, I'll easily listen to hours of my favourite music every day. I love music from pretty much any genere and there's so much amazing music I love.

Some of my favourite artists include:

Red Vox, an indie rock band from New York.
Moe Shop, a French-born Japanese J-Pop artist.
Mcbaise, a French psychedelic rock indie artist.
Lena Raine, an American composer who has worked on big name Video Game soundtracks like Minecraft and Celeste.
Queens of the Stone Age, an American rock band who was formed in the mid-90s.
The Rolling Stones, an widely acclaimed English rock band formed in the early 60s.
Nujabes, a Japanese born composer widely credited for creating the 'lo-fi' genre.
Kendrick Lamar, an American rapper who's regarded to be one of the most important in the genre due to his lyrics and songwriting.
